In a Nutshell

I make delightful product & brand experiences. Currently, I design mobile apps for a range of growing companies at Steamclock Software.

Whether it's creating a brand, product, illustration, you name it – my process is shaped by three C's: curiosity, communication, and collaboration. I use the power of inquiry, investigation, and iteration (and on occasion, alliteration) to design what clients, customers, and stakeholders love and value.

And then I do it all over again – because being able to make awesome things that people love brings me joy.
