Hello, I'm Erica Leong.

Visual Designer + Illustrator / Cat Whisperer + Silly Guy

I'm a visual designer & illustrator, making delightful product and brand experiences for kind folks who care about the details, from startups to SaaS businesses, and more.

A bit more about me

I graduated from Emily Carr University of Art + Design, with a Bachelor's in Communication Design in 2012. Since then, I've spent a decade or so helping clients large and small with all sorts of services, including graphic design, brand identity, illustration, animation, and UI/UX.

As of late, my focus has been in digital product design. I currently work with the lovely team at Steamclock Software, helping design and ship great mobile apps for growing tech companies.

In addition to work, I enjoy drawing, dabbling in code, and learning new skills. I'm also an escape room nerd, D&D enthusiast and boardgame geek – what can I say, I really enjoy creative problem solving.
