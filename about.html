Hello, I'm Erica Leong.

Pronouns: She/Her • Pronounced: Air-wreck-a Lee-ong

I'm a visual designer & illustrator with over {{ 'now' | date: "%Y" | minus: 2013 }} years experience, based in Greater Vancouver, Canada.

I love to create delightful, thoughtful, high-quality product and brand experiences that make a positive difference in peoples' lives.

A bit more about me

In 2012, I graduated from Emily Carr University of Art + Design, with a Bachelor's in Communication Design. I started my career doing graphic design for an indie film studio, and since then have designed brand identities, illustrations, websites, and interfaces for agencies, in-house teams, and via freelance.

As of late, my focus has been in digital product design. I currently work as a senior designer at a software development studio called Steamclock Software, where I lead design on predominantly consumer-facing mobile apps for a range of clients, as well as internal brand & marketing projects.

By nature, I consider myself a craftsperson that cares about getting the details right – whether it’s the copy of a CTA, the bezier curve of micro-animation, or kerning on a wordmark. But as much as I take my work seriously, I also like to play and have fun. Life’s too short to be an asshole, afterall.

Outside of work, you'll likely find me drawing, snowboarding, dodgeball, or playing boardgames with my partner. I'm also a huge nerd when it comes to escape rooms. (What can I say, I really love creative problem solving.)
