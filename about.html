Hello, I'm Erica Leong.

Pronouns: She/Her • Pronounced: Air-wreck-a Lee-ong

I'm a visual designer & illustrator with over {{ 'now' | date: "%Y" | minus: 2013 }} years experience, based in Greater Vancouver, Canada.

I love to create delightful, thoughtful, high-quality product and brand experiences that make a positive mark on peoples' lives.

My Story

In 2012, I graduated from Emily Carr University of Art + Design, with a Bachelor's in Communication Design. I started my career doing graphic design for an indie film studio, and since then have designed brand identities, illustrations, websites, and interfaces for agencies, in-house teams, and via freelance.

As of late, my focus has been in digital product design. I currently work as a senior designer at a software development studio called Steamclock Software, where I lead design on predominantly consumer-facing mobile apps for a range of clients, as well as a handful of internal brand & marketing endeavours.

By nature, I consider myself a craftsperson that cares about getting the details right – whether it’s the copy of a button, the bezier curve of micro-animation, or the kerning of a wordmark. But as much as I take my work seriously, I also value having fun! Life’s too short to be an asshole, afterall.

Outside of work, you'll likely find me learning new things, drawing silly guys, playing dodgeball, D&D, or trying out new boardgames with my partner. I'm also a huge nerd when it comes to escape rooms. (What can I say, I really love creative problem solving.)
