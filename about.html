Hello, I'm Erica Leong.

Visual Designer + Illustrator / Cat Whisperer + Silly Guy

I'm a visual designer & illustrator based in Vancouver, Canada.

I love creating delightful and thoughtful product & brand experiences that make a positive difference in peoples' lives.

A bit more about me

In 2012, I graduated from Emily Carr University of Art + Design, with a Bachelor's in Communication Design. I started my career doing graphic design for an indie film studio, and since then have ventured to doing branding, illustration, websites, and UI/UX for agencies, in-house teams, and freelance.

As of late, my focus has been in digital product design. I currently work as a senior designer at a software development studio called Steamclock Software, where I lead design on a range of mobile apps for growing tech companies, as well as internal endeavours like Steamclock's own apps, marketing, and fine bespoke swag for the team.

Outside of work, I personally love: drawing, snowboarding, dodging balls at dodgeball, and playing boardgames and D&D. I'm also a huge nerd when it comes to escape rooms. What can I say, I really love creative problem solving.
