Hello, I'm Erica Leong.

I'm a visual designer & illustrator with over {{ 'now' | date: "%Y" | minus: 2013 }} years experience, based in Greater Vancouver, Canada.

I love to create delightful, thoughtful, high-quality product and brand experiences that make a positive mark on peoples' lives.

My Story

I started my design career as I'm sure most others have: with a pirated copy of Photoshop at age 15. Shortly after, I studied design more legitimately at Emily Carr University of Art + Design, where I graduated with a Bachelor's of Communication Design in 2012. My first gig was doing graphic design at an indie film studio, and since then I've worked for a range of clients – including tech startups, SaaS companies, financial institutions – offering services in brand design, illustration, website design, and UI/UX.

As of late, my focus has been in digital product design. I currently work as a senior designer at a software development studio called Steamclock Software, where I design predominantly consumer-facing mobile apps for a range of clients, as well as a handful of internal brand & marketing endeavours.

By nature, I consider myself a craftsperson that cares about getting the details right over being right. I value making space for collaboration and feedback, constantly iterating towards great outcomes, and most of all: having fun.

Outside of work, you'll likely find me learning new things, drawing silly guys, playing dodgeball, D&D, or trying out new boardgames with my partner. I'm also a huge nerd when it comes to escape rooms. (What can I say, I really love creative problem solving.)
