Hello, I'm Erica Leong.

Visual Designer + Illustrator / Cat Whisperer + Silly Guy

I'm a visual designer & illustrator, based in Vancouver, Canada.

I make delightful product & brand experiences for kind folks to care about the details.

A bit more about me

In 2012, I graduated from Emily Carr University of Art + Design, with a Bachelor's in Communication Design. Since then, I've spent a decade or so helping clients large and small with all sorts of services, including graphic design, brand identity, illustration, animation, and product design.

As of late, my focus has been in digital product design. I currently work with lovely folks at Steamclock Software as a senior designer, leading design on a range of mobile apps for growing tech companies in the realms of finance, SaaS, consumer brands, and more.

Outside of work, you can find me: drawing silly stuff, fiddling with code, learning new skills, or playing boardgames and TTRPGs with my partner. I'm also a huge nerd when it comes to escape rooms – what can I say, I really love creative problem solving.
