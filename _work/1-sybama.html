    Most business don't actually need a mobile app. Most of the time, a web experience will suffice.

    Goals

    Our goals for this microsite were fairly simple: we wanted to create a small marketing campaign that was fun, memorable, and shareable – geared towards Steamclock's target demographic of CTOs of growing companies. While it could exist standalone, it was also meant to be an extension of the Steamclock's brand values – high quality, playful, nice – which informed the overall playful yet polished look throughout.

    Outcome

    The end result was a microsite that was cheeky and a bit contrarian, but still useful as a tool in the end of the day. Overall, the project was a success: people loved it! New clients have referenced it while reaching out, and we even use it to screen our in-bound leads too.

    A note on tools

    I designed and prototyped the website from wireframes to high-fidelity visuals, along with initial illustrations, using Figma. For some added delight and polish, I used a super combo of Figma, Illustrator, and After Effects to make a Lottie animation for each checklist item.
