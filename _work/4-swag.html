2022 Notebooks

The theme for 2022 was Stay Curious. Metallic purple foil stamp on cobblestone grey vellum, sewn saddle stitch binding.

2021 Notebooks

The theme for 2021 was Craft with Care. Metallic blue foil stamp on light blue vellum, sewn saddle stitch binding.

2021 Holiday Cards

In 2021, we made limited edition physical NFTs (as a joke, of course). Holographic foil stamp on white cotton stock.

2020 Holiday Cards

In 2020... well, we all know what happened. So we wanted to bring some warmth and levity to our clients. Vintage gold foil stamp on white cotton stock.

2019 Holiday Cards

2019 was the first year we decided to explore cool options for our print work. Two colour letterpress on white cotton stock.

Printing for all of these were done by the stellar folks at Porchlight Press.
