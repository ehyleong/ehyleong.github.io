2022 Notebooks

In 2022, Steamclock's slogan for the year was "Stay Curious."

After brainstorming various ways to convey curiosity, we landed on making a museum of crafted curios – loaded with a bunch of fun, interesting items and easter eggs.

These were printed with metallic purple foil stamp on cobblestone grey vellum, sewn saddle stitch binding.

2021 Notebooks

In 2021, Steamclock's slogan for the year was "Craft with Care."

These were printed with metallic purple foil stamp on cobblestone grey vellum, sewn saddle stitch binding.

2021 Holiday Cards

In 2021, we made limited edition physical NFTs (as a joke, of course).

Holographic foil stamp on white cotton stock.

2020 Holiday Cards

In 2020... well, we all know what happened. So we wanted to bring some warmth and levity to our clients. Vintage gold foil stamp on white cotton stock.

2019 Holiday Cards

2019 was the first year we decided to invest in doing letterpress for our holiday cards. Two colour letterpress on white cotton stock.

Printing for all of these were done by the stellar folks at Porchlight Press.
